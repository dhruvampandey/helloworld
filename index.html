Dr. Dhruvam Pandey

Research Scientist at CREOL

I am currently working as a Postdoctoral Scholar in the Han Lab at CREOL, The College of Optics and Photonics, University of Central Florida. My research focuses on super-resolution microscopy, high-throughput imaging, and quantitative phase imaging, with an emphasis on both computational and experimental approaches. I have completed my PhD under Prime Minister's Research Fellows (PMRF) scheme from the Department of Electrical Engineering (Photonics) at Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur. I work with Prof. G Rajshekhar in the domain of optical coherent imaging. Here, we are mainly interested in quantitatively measuring the phase of the optical wave-field scattered from an object or specimen. We work on both the experimental and computational aspects of coherent imaging. The applications of the domain includes Non-destructive Precision Metrology, Nanoscale Topography, 3D Shape Detection and Cell Imaging.
